I am in my early 40’s with 2 lovely kids and the love handles (presents from my kids) that is so hard to get rid of.

I tried to diet and exercise, but I guess I wasn’t very determined so the result wasn’t satisfactory.

I heard CoolSculpting and consulted a few different places and thought I might have to use some extra help to get rid of the stubborn fat in my abdominal and flank areas.

Although “MD Laser, Skin, & Vein” is quite far from where I leave, I chose to work with Kim Lang after a phone conversation with her. Kim is very helpful on the phone; she provided all the information I needed on the phone very patiently and professionally.

My experience with her during the treatments was even better. She accommodated my schedules and started my treatments earlier than usual so that I could go back to work after the treatments. She is a very pleasant and nice lady; and she responded to the calls very quickly during the treatments.

I am also very impressed by the nice environment and extremely helpful and friendly staff in the office. I remembered a wrong date for one of my appointments, and the lady at the front desk coordinated with a few people and squeezed my appointment in.

All the healthcare professionals I talked with (Kim Long, an MA, and an MD) are very friendly and knowledgeable.

I totally received two treatments, 1 month apart between the two. In the meantime, I better followed a healthy diet and exercise routine. Now I am quite happy with the results, and feel even more motivated to follow the healthy routine to keep the momentum going.
