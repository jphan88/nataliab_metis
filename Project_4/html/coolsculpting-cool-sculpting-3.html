Cool sculpting

  • 20 Oct 2017
  • 3 months post
I began my fitness journey at the beginning of 2017. I’m loving my butt, legs and arms but I’m not loving my midsection. So I had cool sculpting done 3 months ago but I don’t see a difference. I was told to expect good results since they overlapped on some of my stomach but I’m pretty disappointed with the results.
