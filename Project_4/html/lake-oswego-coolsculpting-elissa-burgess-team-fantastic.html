After getting back in shape after having two...

  • 23 May 2016
  • 10 months post
After getting back in shape after having two children I still had a little “pooch” around my abdomen. No matter how much I dieted and exercised, the pooch remained. I heard about CoolSculpting through friends and decided to have a consultation with Dr. Elissa Burgess and her team. During my consultation I also asked about the same type of problem area on my arms. After learning I was a good candidate for both areas, I underwent one treatment on my abdomen and two on my arms. I and am thrilled with the results and am much more comfortable and confident in tighter clothing. During my follow up visit I asked about subsequent treatments but was told that I didn’t have enough "pinchable" fat left. While some other docs may have gladly taken my money and performed an additional treatment, Dr. Burgess and her team were honest in telling me the benefit, if any, of an additional treatment wouldn’t be worth the cost. I appreciate the honesty by Dr. Burgess and her team and this is why she has earned my trust and ongoing business. You won’t find a more professional doctor and entire office staff than that of Burgess Plastic Surgery. I have had several procedures performed by Dr. Burgess and her team (breast aug, fillers, botox, chemical peels, and CoolSculpting) and am always so appreciative of their honesty and conservative approach.
